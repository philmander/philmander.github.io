About Me

I’m Phil Mander and I focus on making cutting-edge web based products. I specialize in HTML5 technologies, most notably Javascript on the client and the server.

After graduating with a degree in Internet Computing, I moved to London where I cut my teeth as a web developer working for CMC Markets, working on business critical international web sites and applications. I moved to Amsterdam in 2011 to work at Backbase where I have worked in a variety of roles at an exciting rapidly growing financial software company.

I also have a much experience working with agile development methodologies in real-world projects. I’m a certified scrum master and scrum product owner.

I’m now dividing my time between working part time as a frontend architect at Backbase, freelance projects and my own open source projects. I’m available for hire

When I’m not doing the above, I keep myself busy practising Judo, running, painting or helping my wife, Lora, with her theatre company, Orange Tea Theatre
